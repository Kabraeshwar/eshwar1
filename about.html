About Us

We are a happy school that provides a safe and nurturing environment of learning which enables all our children to achieve the skills needed for adult life. Through a stimulating engaging curriculum that encompasses indoor learning opportunities, we insure that every day counts at Jijau School & Jr. College. We pride ourselves on our Values Based Education which results in a welcoming and caring ethos. We are all learners and our key values underpin our success, inspiring children, parents, staff and governors to learn and grow together. We enjoy working closely with parents and recognise the importance of establishing strong home/school partnerships. We understand that the health and safety of all our children is of paramount importance so our school provides a safe environment in which our children can flourish. We hope you will enjoy our school you get a flavor for the opportunities we provide at “Jijau School & Jr. College Shelgaon”.
